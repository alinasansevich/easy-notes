EasyNotes is a web-based sticky notes tool that lets you easily add notes on a digital board.

Give a title to your notes, write them as just text or as a to-do list. It keeps all your notes listed so you can quickly see the contents of each one and find the one you want.

Incorporate EasyNotes into your workflow for study or work, keep all your notes organized in one virtual board and take them with you wherever you go. Leave all clutter behind.
