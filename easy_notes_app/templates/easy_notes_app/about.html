EasyNotes is a web-based sticky notes tool that lets you easily add notes on a digital board.

You can give a title to your notes, write them in a text field or add a bullet list. It keeps all your notes listed so you can quickly see the contents of the note and find the one you want. You can incorporate EasyNotes into your workflow for study or work, increasing your productivity and eliminating procrastination. keep all your notes organized in one virtual board, take them with you, and enjoy your day! Leave all clutter behind.
